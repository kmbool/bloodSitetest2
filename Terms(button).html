Conditions for donating blood:

  1. The donor should be in good health and not suffer from any infectious diseases.
  2. The age of the donor should be between 18-65 years.
  3. The donor's weight must be at least 50 kg.
  4. That the hemoglobin percentage for men is 14-17 gm, and for women 12-14 gm.
  5. The pulse is between 50-100 per minute.
